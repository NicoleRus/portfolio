Santa of Texas Website

Santa of Texas Website

Purpose

This website served to draw a larger audience for Santa David. Launched right before Christmas time, the website gave him the online presence he needed.

My Role

I was hired by Santa David to put together this site using a marketing eye. Although digital marketing is not my area of expertise, we worked with one another and others in the field to put forth an attractive website. The website enables Santa David to tell people where he will be and when, as well as allow them to book him for an event. The site is Wordpress-based, but many features, such as the Countdown to Christmas and the share modal were built by me using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This project is ongoing and we are currently working on setting up SEO strategies to implement in the near future. We expect the site to make a significant difference for Santa David by Christmas 2019.
